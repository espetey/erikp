About Me

My name is Erik Peterson. I am an MBA student and I love to build useful products that make people's lives better. I have experience across all roles in a product's life cycle, from software engineer and IT support to designer and content creator. I am interested in learning and measuring why customers fall in love with some products and not others. I also earned a master's degree in English studying American literature, Native American comics and graphic novels, and British literature.

I cook, bake, run, ride bikes, garden, draw, paint, play guitar, and listen to music proficiently. I prefer warm, humid weather between 84° and 97° F, latitudes in the low 30°'s, and the months of June, July, August, and September.
